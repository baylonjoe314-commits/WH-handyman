The Working Hands Process

Clear steps, transparent budgets, and respectful crews. You get updates before you ask and a walkthrough before we leave.

  • 1. Site Visit & Quote: We inspect, measure, and send a detailed scope with options.
  • 2. Prep & Protection: Floors, furniture, and vents are protected before any demolition or sanding.
  • 3. Craft the Details: Skilled hands execute, keeping the space tidy with daily check-ins.
  • 4. Final Walkthrough: We inspect with you, handle touch-ups, and leave the site broom clean.
